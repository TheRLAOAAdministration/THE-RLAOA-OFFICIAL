1. Introduction

This book outlines the laws and regulations governing conduct and behavior on Roblox to ensure a safe and enjoyable environment for all users. Adherence to these laws is mandatory for all users of the Roblox platform.

2. General Conduct

Article 1: Harassment and Bullying

Prohibits any form of harassment, bullying, or threatening behavior towards other users.

Article 2: Hate Speech and Discrimination

Bans language or content promoting hate, violence, or discrimination based on race, ethnicity, religion, gender, sexual orientation, or disability.

Article 3: Inappropriate Content

Forbids content that is sexually explicit, obscene, or otherwise inappropriate for children.

Article 4: Online Dating

Prohibits engaging in or promoting romantic or dating interactions on the platform.

Article 5: Using Inappropriate Avatar Customizations

Restricts avatar customizations that are offensive or inappropriate.

3. Content and Intellectual Property

Article 6: Intellectual Property Infringement

Prohibits the use or upload of content that infringes on the intellectual property rights of others.

Article 7: Creating Dangerous Content

Bans creating or sharing content that could potentially cause harm or danger to users.

Article 8: Creating Violent Content

Prohibits content that is excessively violent or graphic.

Article 9: Using Unauthorized Content

Bans the use of unauthorized or pirated content in games or experiences.

Article 10: Creating Inappropriate Game Themes

Restricts game themes that are offensive or inappropriate.

4. Game Mechanics and Exploitation

Article 11: Exploiting and Cheating

Prohibits the use of cheats, exploits, or hacks to gain unfair advantages.

Article 12: Manipulating Game Mechanics

Bans exploiting or manipulating game mechanics for unfair advantages.

Article 13: Creating Disruptive Game Elements

Restricts designing game elements that disrupt gameplay or user experience.

Article 14: Using Unauthorized Bots

Prohibits the use of bots or automation tools not authorized by Roblox.
